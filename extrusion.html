Extrusion

PreviousNext
Extrusion is somewhat of a puzzle game, where you click spaces on the board to turn the surrounding spaces your color, with the eventual goal being to have more squares of your color filling the area. However, there is a computer-controlled player who is trying to do the same. The behavior of the computer changes with each level. First, it mirrors your moves, doing them on its own side of the board. Then it reflects them, doing the opposite of what you do. Finally, it makes its own moves, trying to maximize the amount of squares it can turn its color.

Role

Lead Programmer

Tool

Perlenspiel

What I Did on This Project

  • Created the first two AI stages, which take the player's previous move, apply the current stage's rules, and make its own. If the exact move can't be made, the closest one will be
  • Created the third AI stage, which takes the state of the board, figures out which move will result in the most squares turned its color, and then makes it
  • Created the UI elements that show how many turns are left in a level, which player is winning in terms of color amount, and the transition animation that plays as stages change
