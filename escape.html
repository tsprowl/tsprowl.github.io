Role: Lead Programmer

  • Escape From Knossos was a game made for Professor Moriarty's Digital Game Design II using his custom Perlenspiel engine. It had a prompt of taking a legend, tale, or piece of folklore, and transform it into a story-based game. The catch was that we could only use the very limited text bar at the top to tell the story.
  • As the lead programmer, I created the levels and mechanics, implemented the audio, created the AI for the minotaur, and created the procedural generation for both the background colors and the seagull spawning in the final level.
