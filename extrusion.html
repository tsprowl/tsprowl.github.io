Role: Lead Programmer

  • Extrusion was a game made for Professor Moriarty's Digital Game Design I using his custom Perlenspiel engine. The goal to was to make a game that embodied wisdom, however we may take it.
  • "By three methods we may learn wisdom: First, by reflection, which is noblest; Second, by imitation, which is easiest; and third by experience, which is the bitterest." - Confucius
  • We also had design constraints to not have any text outside of the title, use only the mouse, have a set maximum grid size, and no colors besides black, white, and shades of gray.
  • As the lead programmer, I was responsible for the scoring system and the opponent AI. The AI follows the principles of wisdom put forward by Confucius in each level. First it mirrors (reflects) the player, then it imitates (copies), then it uses its own strategy (creates it's own experience).
