Assignment 2

For this project, I wanted to do a couple things artistically. First, I had little control over the color of my first one, so I wanted to be able to actually get that functioning, and be able to precisely control color. I'm also a big fan of, to put it simply, making things corrupt. To this end, the noise inclusion was always going to be interesting, and I wanted to be able to control the individual factors to try and put together a cursed filter for the live video.

Technically, I wanted to implement fractal brownian motion, since that was one of the more interesting types of noise to me. I aso wanted to condense the work into a single file, purely because I am still somewhat new at HTML and linking things over files scares me slightly. I originally also wanted to either have the fractal noise shifting over time, resulting in a non-static change, or have the noise applied directly to the video itself, resulting in a warped and distorted video. As it stands, I was unable to get that done, but it might be a potential improvement in the future

The feedback from the classmate was rather positive. He mentioned how he said that he could make "some very nicely cursed photos", so that aspect was certainly a success. He said it reminded him of photos in a horror game with a dirt grain filter with extreme color adjustments and inversions. His critique was that, while the crunchy noise was a cool look, it would be cooler to lean into it more by having the noise change over time. I couldn't agree more, so I might end up fiddling with that more in my spare time.
