Blossom

PreviousNext
Blossom is a platforming game where you play as a little flower creature navigating three different levels to collect mystical items known as Dewdrops. In each level, you must navigate a variety of obstacles by jumping, swinging, and floating your way from platform to platform.

Roles

Programmer, Audio Engineer, Level Designer

Tools

Unreal Engine 4, Reaper

What I Did on This Project

  • Implemented the basic system of movement and jumping
  • Sourced sound effects from online and personal libraries, modified them to suit our needs, and implemented them within Unreal
  • Took assets and outlines from the art team and turned them into levels within the game
