Role: Lead Programmer

  • Push It! was a game made for Professor Moriarty's Digital Game Design I using his custom Perlenspiel engine. This was the first game we made in the engine, and our only direction was to create a puzzle game. My partner Raul and I decided to make a crate pushing game to recall Flash games of old, with a hard and easy mode.
  • As the lead programmer, I was responsible for player movement, the implementation of the levels, and configuring the win conditions.
