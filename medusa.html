Medusa

PreviousNext

Roles: Lead Programmer

  • Medusa is a 2D, top-down RPG that puts a spin on the classic tale of Medusa. You first play as the hero Perseus, on a quest to slay the wicked Medusa. As you deal the killing blow, you are transported back in time to experience Medusa's story through her eyes, giving new context and a new light to the famous Greek myth.
  • This game was made as a joint Major Qualifying Project, combining game design, psychology, and philosophy to create a game with a goal to create a game that would generate empathy through perspective-taking. Our group created the game in tandem with a psych study testing the effects of our game on empathy, hostile sexism, and rape myth acceptance.
  • As the lead, and only, programmer on Medusa, I was responsible for all the underlying mechanics in the game, including combat, enemy AI, the dialogue system, Medusa's stone vision, and implementation of all the cutscenes.
