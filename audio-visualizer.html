Hellish Audio Visualizer

This project is an audio visualizer created using HTML and JavaScript. It extracts the data of the song in question, in the case 'BFG Division' from DOOM 2016, analyzes it, separates it into bins based on its frequency, and these bins are then transformed into the bars we see. Users can also change the three levels of color displayed along the bars, as well as the colors displayed in those sections and in the background.
