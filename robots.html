aMAZEing Robots

aMAZEing Robots is a game prototype developed by the Intentional Design Studio for FIRST Robotics. Up to four players take control of autonomous robots who must work together to swap parts and remove obstacles in order to fix their space station. While this may sound like teamwork is the best option, only one robot will be able to take credit for saving the day, meaning competition is also a factor.

Role

Network & Gameplay Programmer

Tool

Unity

What I Did on This Project

  • Created the systems for players to pick up, drop, and use tools on the environment to remove obstacles
  • Ensured that game state changes were reflected across all players on a wireless network, including tool pickups and drops, obstacle removal, and player movement
