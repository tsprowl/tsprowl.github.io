Stack Attack

Feel like you're on top of the world

Previous Next

Role: Lead Programmer

  • Stack Attack was a game made for Professor Moriarty's Digital Game Design II using his custom Perlenspiel engine. It had a prompt of making a game that could be played on both mobile and PC. My partner and I decided to make a game that was simple and relaxing. The catch was that we could only use the very limited text bar at the top to tell the story.
  • As the lead programmer, I created the block stacking and the procedural color generation for the blocks and for the background, as they try to contrast as much as possible.
