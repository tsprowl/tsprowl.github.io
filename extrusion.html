Extrusion

PreviousNext

Role: Lead Programmer

  • Extrusion is a game designed for PC where the player competes against an AI opponent to create the most blocks of their color within a certain amount of moves. It was made for Professor Moriarty's Digital Game Design I class using his custom Perlenspiel engine, with the goal to make a game that embodied wisdom.
  • By three methods we may learn wisdom: First, by reflection, which is noblest; Second, by imitation, which is easiest; and third by experience, which is the bitterest." - Confucius
  • As the lead programmer, I was responsible for the scoring system and the opponent AI. The AI follows the principles of wisdom put forward by Confucius in each level. First it mirrors (reflects) the player, then it imitates (copies), then it uses its own strategy (creates its own experience).
